維修知識Repair Tips

學多啲・整少啲Learn More, Fix Less

實用手機保養知識，等部機襟用啲。 Practical phone care tips to make your device last longer.

1

點樣避免 Samsung FRP 鎖？How to Avoid Samsung FRP Lock

Factory Reset Protection 係 Google 嘅安全功能。還原出廠設定前，記得先喺設定入面移除 Google 帳號，否則 reset 後就會彈 FRP 鎖要入返舊帳號密碼先開到機。

Factory Reset Protection is a Google security feature. Before factory resetting, always remove your Google account in Settings first, or you'll be locked out after reset.

🔓 FRP🔓 Security
2

手機電池點樣先用得耐？Make Your Battery Last Longer

鋰電池最理想嘅電量範圍係 20%-80%。唔好成日用到 0% 先充，亦唔好長期 100% 插住充過夜。避免過熱（例如唔好擺喺車廂暴曬），電池壽命會長好多。

Lithium batteries work best between 20%-80%. Don't drain to 0% often, and avoid keeping it at 100% plugged in overnight. Keep it cool — heat is the #1 battery killer.

🔋 電池🔋 Battery
3

跌爆 Mon 之後應該點做？What To Do After a Broken Screen

第一時間貼上保護貼防止玻璃碎跌出同進一步裂開。如果觸控仍然正常，可以繼續用住先，但建議盡快更換，避免碎玻璃整壞液晶層，到時就要成個 mon 總成更換，價錢更貴。

Apply a screen protector immediately to prevent glass shards. If touch still works, you can still use it, but replace soon — cracked glass can damage the LCD underneath, requiring a full assembly replacement (more expensive).

🖥️ 螢幕🖥️ Screen
4

手機充唔到電？先試呢幾樣Phone Not Charging? Try This First

先用牙籤輕輕清下充電口嘅塵垢（好多時係入咗毛塵）。換第二條線試下。如果都唔得，可能係尾插壞咗，攞上嚟我哋幫你check。

First, gently clean the charging port with a toothpick (lint buildup is very common). Try a different cable. If still not charging, the charging port may need replacement — bring it in and we'll check.

🔌 充電🔌 Charging
